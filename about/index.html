I am a software engineer. I am doing web development using .net and angular/recat mostly.

I try to understand the business domain and purposes of a project where I work. It helps me to understand better the essence of the product and continuously improve it.

I pay lot of attention on code quality and building reliable software solutions. In addition, I love sharing my knowledge and enjoy playing a mentor role.

My own projects#

  • Tech.Interviewer - a platform for collecting and analyzing IT salaries in Kazakhstan. Helps people to understand the market and make better salary decisions. In 2025, a section for company reviews was added.
  • EV Charge Tracker - iOS app for tracking EV (Electric Vehicle) charging and other type of expenses costs. Built with SwiftUI and Swift using Claude.
  • Individual Entrepreneur in Kazakhstan - a knowledge base about Individual Entrepreneur (ru: “ИП”) in Kazakhstan. It helps people to understand the process of registration and running IE.
